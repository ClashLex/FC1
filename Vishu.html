Happy Vishu 🌼

Vishu marks the Malayalam New Year, symbolizing prosperity, hope, and new beginnings in Kerala.

Traditions

Vishukkani

A sacred arrangement viewed first in the morning for good luck.

Konna Flowers

Golden flowers that symbolize prosperity and happiness.

Oil Lamps

Lamps are lit to bring positivity and divine blessings.

Celebrations

Vishu Sadya

A grand feast served on banana leaves with traditional dishes.

Kaineettam

Elders give money to younger ones as blessings.

Fireworks

Celebrations include crackers and joyful gatherings.
